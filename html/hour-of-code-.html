In a bid to foster a passion for computer science and programming among students, Our team hosted an Hour of Code event. The primary objective was to introduce fundamental algorithms, provide insights into computer science, and equip students with the basics of programming. The event kicked off with an informative session where students were introduced to the world of algorithms and programming. Emphasizing the importance of these skills in today's technology-driven world, participants were given a glimpse into the vast possibilities that the field of computer science offers. A key highlight of the event was the active participation of students in solving diverse puzzles and engaging with the course materials available on the code.org platform. This hands-on approach not only made learning interactive but also allowed students to apply the concepts they had just learned in a practical setting. As the Hour of Code event unfolded, students demonstrated enthusiasm and eagerness to grasp the intricacies of coding. The atmosphere was charged with a collective spirit of curiosity and exploration, as participants delved into the world of algorithms and programming languages. In recognition of their commitment and successful completion of the coding challenges, students were awarded certificates at the conclusion of the event. The certificates not only served as a testament to their newfound knowledge but also as a source of encouragement to further pursue their interest in computer science.

Student's Performance
